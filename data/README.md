# Dataset

This project uses open-access Medical AI research papers collected from PubMed Central (PMC) Open Access.

The documents consist of peer-reviewed research articles related to:

- Medical Imaging
- Deep Learning
- Machine Learning
- Computer Vision
- Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

All papers remain the property of their original publishers and authors.

The PDFs are stored in:

data/raw/

and are processed automatically by the ingestion pipeline.
